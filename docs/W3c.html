World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)

What's is w3c?

According to w3.org, the w3c is: "an international community where Member organizations, a full-time staff, and the public work together to develop Web standards. Led by Web inventor and Director Tim Berners-Lee and CEO Jeffrey Jaffe, W3C's mission is to lead the Web to its full potential."
